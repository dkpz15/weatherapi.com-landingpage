Frequently Asked Questions

1 API Call/Request is a request made to our API server through HTTP/S to get data.

We get our weather data from thousands of global private and public weather stations and weather data providers in the world.

Realtime weather is updated every 10-15 minute. Whereas forecast weather data is updated every 4-6 hours in a day.

Our historical weather data is made up of forecast data and not from actuals. Data is archived just after midnight for the previous day. Historical data is available from 1 Jan, 2010 till now.

Payments can be made via Paypal and Credit/Debit card (Visa, MasterCard, Discover, Diner's Club, American Express). You may request annual bank transfer payments to be enabled. You can change your payment method at any given time in the "Payment" section of your account dashboard.

If you go over your calls or request per month then you will stop receiving data for that month. You may upgrade to higher plan at any time and no changes are required at your end and the API key stays the same. The quota is reset at midnight on 1st of each month UTC.
